Félix Albertos Marco

Félix Albertos Marco started working at the University of Castilla-La Mancha (UCLM) in 2009. He worked at the Informatics Research Institute of Albacete (I3A) (Department of Computer Systems, Factulty of Computer Science Engineering).

He developed his work in the UCLM in the Interactive Systems Engineering (ISE) research group (formerly known as Interactive Systems Everywhere).

Since 2014 until 2018 he was funded by a fellowship at the University of Castilla-La Mancha.

He made his predoctoral stay in the Informatics Research Institute of Toulouse (IRIT) University Paul Sabatier (Toulouse 3), Toulouse (France), within the research group Interactive Critical Systems (ICS).

He received his PhD with his thesis entitled Responsive Web Interaction from the University of Castilla-La Mancha in 2017.

In 2018 he made a postdoctoral stay at the Universit Nice Sophia Antipolis, hosted by the CNRS research unit Laboratoire d’Informatique, Signaux et Systmes de Sophia Antipolis (I3S), where he is an external collaborator within the Scalable and Pervasive softwARe and Knowledge Systems (SPARKS) team.

In 2018 he started working in the University of Lleida (UdL) in the Grup de Recerca en Interacció Persona Ordinador i Integraci de Dades ( Griho). In 2020 he won the position of assistant professor in UdL.

Currently, since 2021, he works as an assistant professor in the UCLM, where he got a position in the Facultad CC. Sociales y Tecnologías de la Información of Talavera de la Reina, Departamento de Tecnologías y Sistemas de Información (DTSI).

In 2022 he made a research stay at the Université Côte d’Azur (formerly knwon as Université Nice Sophia Antipolis) where he joined the SPARKS/WIMMICS team (Web-Instrumented Man-Machine Interactions, Communities and Semantics).
